About Chen Wei

I am outgoing and always have a smile on my face.

I make life an adventure, and have some amazing memories to show for it. I push myself to explore and go outside of my comfort zone – good things are always on the horizon.

OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES

In nature I’ve found many opportunities to take up the mantle of adventure. Much of my love for the outdoors comes from many quiet nights spent in national parks around the world.

FAITH

Along my journey through life, I became a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The friends I’ve made there, and the family-oriented teachings, have massively improved my life.

LEARNING

I’m passionate about learning new things, and applying it to my craft. Right now I spend time learning Website development and English, with the hope that someday I can embark from Taiwan and work as a front-end Engineer for a company in another country.

TRAVELING

I’ve ventured far away from my native country of Taiwan, taking solo backpacking trips to Japan, Malaysia, and the United States. While I enjoy the solitude of traveling alone and discovering breathtaking view all to myself, I’ve also met some amazing people and built lasting friendships along the way.
